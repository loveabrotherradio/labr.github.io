    love a brother radio is a collective non-commercial, non-corporate, non-monetised stream for house and other dance genres as well as jazz, funk, hip-hop and any music that inspire us to live, love or act. We are dedicated to a no drama home for tunes, social conscious and fun. We use non-monetised platforms, zero ads (including web or stream analytics) and open source tools wherever practical. Any of our original content is CC-SA for non-commercial use as is that of many of the community programmes and music we share. We are in a live testing phase and have the above listed live shows on the "air".

    LABR of LOVE mixes

    0300 and 2200 GMT everyday enjoy a growing selection of custom deep and soulful house music mixes from long-time house veteren DJ Brother Soul

    *LIVE* midweek workday chill

    1600 GMT Wednesdays. DJ Brother Soul hooks you up with a less intense mix of jams for some inconspicuous chair dancing at your desk, head nodding in the car or wherever you might be.

    *LIVE* weekend workout

    2000 GMT Fridays. DJ Brother Soul helps you kick off your weekend with a banging house mix.

    UpFront Soul

    Funk, soul, and jazz: UpFront Soul with Sanguine Fromage of WERU-FM. 120 minutes of soulful sounds to which you may get down.

    Frequency Theory

    Frequency theory is funk, jazz, hip-hop, and jelly donuts shaken from policemen's hands by naked mole rats decked out in paisley swimshorts. From Radio Free Brooklyn

    Out of the Woods

    Mostly acoustic music from Jon 'Chip' Colcord.

    Reggae Explorations

    Hosted by Mike Roots form WAPJ, “Reggae Explorations” and previously “Positive Vibrations” going strong since 1997

    Planetary Radio

    Sometimes we like to get our rocket science on, you should too. Created and hosted by the Planetary Society.

    Making Contact

    Social conscious programmes from theradioproject.org
